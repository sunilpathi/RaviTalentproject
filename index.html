Welcome to

RAVI TALENT SCHOOL

RAVI TALENT SCHOOL BOLLARAM was established in 2005 and is managed by Pvt. Unaided. Located in Jinnaram block of Medak district, Telangana, the school offers classes from Grade 1 to 7 with co-educational and pre-primary sections.

The campus includes 7 classrooms, electricity, playground, drinking water, and computer-based learning. The school ensures a safe and supportive environment for every student.
