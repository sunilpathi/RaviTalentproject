About Our School

Building Strong Foundations Since 2005

RAVI Talent School Bollaram

RAVI TALENT SCHOOL BOLLARAM was established in 2005 and is managed by Pvt. Unaided. Located in I.D.A Bollaram, Sangareddy, Telangana, the school offers classes from Grade 1 to 7. It is a co-educational institution with an attached pre-primary section.

The school is approachable by all-weather roads and follows an academic session starting in April. English is the medium of instruction, ensuring students receive quality education with modern standards.

The school has a private building with 7 classrooms in good condition, along with 2 additional rooms for non-teaching activities and a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher.
